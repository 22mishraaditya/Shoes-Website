AboutUs

At Nike, we believe in the power of sport to move the world forward. Founded in 1964 as Blue Ribbon Sports and officially becoming Nike, Inc. in 1971, we've grown from a small distributor of Japanese running shoes to a global leader in athletic footwear, apparel, and equipment. Our iconic Swoosh logo represents motion and speed, reflecting our commitment to innovation and excellence.

Today, Nike serves athletes of all levels in over 190 countries, with groundbreaking technologies like Air Max cushioning, Flyknit construction, and Dri-FIT moisture-wicking fabric. From the basketball court to the running track, from yoga studios to city streets, Nike products help athletes push their limits and achieve their goals. Our mission remains simple: To bring inspiration and innovation to every athlete in the world.
